Gandhi Jayanti 2014 : Peace March and Anuraag festival

PEACE MARCH

For the third year in succession, Shishuvan is organizing a Peace March on Thursday, 2nd October, 2014, in order to reaffirm and celebrate Gandhi's values. The theme of the march is "Cleanliness" in actions, words and deeds.

At the Peace March, we can display banners/placards/flags with slogans/quotes that talk about values that we believe in and the work that we are doing which will make a difference to the country and the world at large. The march can be silent or with bhajans or with drums.

The assembly time is 7.00 am sharp at Shishuvan school. The March will begin at 7.30 am sharp and proceed on a earmarked route around Matunga (Central Railway), covering distance of 4.5 Km and end by 9:00 am.

1. Experiential Learning Workshops from Ecomantra
Eco-workshops from Ecomantra's experiential learning division called ECOLE offers eco-friendly nature based workshops. These workshops are hands-on, interesting and often new for participants. Fun and learn is the motto of these workshops, while the subtle message of conservation is conveyed in every workshop.

1 (a) Forgotten Village Games
Everyone loves a game. In this program, we will explore traditional village games that are slowly getting lost. No one in the city plays these games anymore and villagers too have moved onto modern forms of entertainment. Including games played by their ancestors, these games will be taught by villagers themselves in the school. Some of the countryside games include different ways of playing marbles, some earthy fun games, team based games and much more! Have fun while you learn interesting aspects of gaming from our heritage.

1 (b) Animal Bonding
Our classic nature based activity, this time we concentrate on small creatures that live around us, yet we hardly seem to notice them or know about them. Our city is full of living creatures some wild, some even kept as pets. In this activity, we encourage children and parents to bond with the animals, insects, etc by learning to empathise with them. Insects like ants, caterpillars, and small creatures like snails, tadpoles, ducklings or chicks, baby guinea pigs, baby rabbits, any rescued animal ( if any during that time period) etc. will be part of this exciting program. An expert naturalist and animal rescue resource will be part of this program. * Parents can accompany the children below 5 years of age.

1 (c) Calligraphy for beginners: Chancery Cursive Style
Calligraphy is an interesting art involving lettering and helps make our words interesting to read. In this workshop, we will learn the chancery cursive style of calligraphy while understanding the alphabets, space between words, lines and paragraph.
Material required to be carried by students: 80/100 page centimeter graph book, 2.0 calligraphy pen, A4 size colourful sheets, pencil, eraser, scale, colourful sketch pens.

1 (d) Advance Calligraphy:
Taking our knowledge of Calligraphy forward, we will practice our newly learnt art on various interesting objects in daily use like an umbrella, mug, cloth, etc.
Material to be carried: Plain white T-Shirt, Plain Umbrella, Plain Mug, Permanent marker pen with chisel cut, fabric colours, flat brushes, palette, water container, rags, old news papers, ceramic paints.

10. Science workshops by Beyond Academics:
'Beyond Academics' is a fun learning company and is dedicated to organizing educational field-trips and unique workshops/events for schools in Mumbai. Our main aim is to assist schools in imparting 'out of classroom' practical training in a fun and exciting environment.
Science is Magic, Science is Fun! Beyond Academics brings to you an innovative program where children will gain knowledge of concepts of Science thru fun filled experiments, hands on activities and much more. The aim of the program is to kindle reasoning and logical thinking amongst kids and also give them a sense of achievement thru the hands on activities.
The workshop will be conducted by trained teachers and will include orientation of basic concepts, activity kit for each child, Q&A session with the experts and fun time with their friends! Each participant will get to perform their own experiments with the help of the kit under the guidance of a highly professional team.

10 (a) Science Workshop (Juniors) on Sound:
Orientation of basic concepts- What is sound, how sound is produced, how sound propagates or travels, properties of sound, how ear works.
Experiments:
1. Screaming balloon (To prove that vibrations cause sound)- Participants understand that vibrations cause sound. Vibrations are not visible to naked eyes but only if there are vibrations sound can be produced.
2. Larynx laughs - How sound is produced by humans. Participants experience vibration while speaking. Sound that is produced by humans are also due to vibrations. Sound travels through a medium and only then it is heard.
3. Make whistles - Fun experiments. Participants apply the knowledge of vibrations to make toys that produce sound.

10 (b) Science Workshop (Seniors) on Centre of Gravity
This workshop orients about the basic concepts of Centre of Gravity, how our body balances itself.
Experiments:
1. Balancing nails (Group activity)- Participants face a unique challenge of being able to balance many nails on 1 nail. Knowledge of center of gravity makes it easy to balance up to 10 nails on 1 nail.
2. Make a bird that supports itself on its beak- Take home toy made by the participants. A bird balances itself on its beak without any other support
3. Unusual human body- Human body balances itself using many tricks that we fail to notice. Some experiences to participants to understand this better.
